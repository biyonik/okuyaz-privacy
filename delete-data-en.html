üì± Deleting Local Data

To delete all OkuYaz data stored on your device:

1 On Android Devices

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Go to Apps or App Management
  3. Find and tap OkuYaz
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear data or Delete all data
  6. Confirm

Or: Completely uninstall the app:

  1. Long press on the OkuYaz icon
  2. Select Uninstall or Remove
  3. Confirm

üí° When you uninstall the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

‚òÅÔ∏è Deleting Cloud Data (Optional)

If you enabled cloud backup with Google Play Games, you need to delete this data separately:

1 Delete Google Play Games Data

  1. Open the Google Play Games app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Select Delete Play Games account and data
  5. Delete data for OkuYaz

2 Alternative Method

  1. Go to Google Account Settings
  2. Navigate to Data and privacy
  3. Select Data from apps and services you use
  4. Find and delete OkuYaz data

üí≥ Purchase Data

Purchases made through Google Play are stored in your Google account. This data:

  • Remains in your purchase history (unless refunded or canceled)
  • Restores your premium status if you reinstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed or modified by OkuYaz

To delete your purchase data, contact Google Play Support.
